Rules

Welcome to tic tac slot! It pretty much plays like tic tac toe with a few key differances

  • click the red lever to start with X going first or the blue lever to start with O
  • On your turn you need to stop the reel on your symbol or a double symbol
  • The game ends when the all reels have been stopped
  • Reels you score are only counted towards your point total if the rest of the line is either your symbol or doubles
  • Like in Tic Tac Toe lines can be scored horizontaly, verticaly, or diagonaly
  • Each symbol you get is 100 points and each double in a line doubles the total points in a line
  • Lines that are all doubles go to no one
